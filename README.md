# Decoding the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election
## A Fact-Based Data Analytics Project | AtliQ Media

### Project Overview
This project analyses the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 
results across all 234 constituencies, comparing outcomes 
with the 2021 election to identify patterns of electoral 
realignment.

### Key Findings
- 163 out of 234 constituencies changed their winning party
- 69.7% of Tamil Nadu's political map was redrawn in one election
- TVK won 108 seats drawing from DMK (65), AIADMK (26) and INC (11)
- Chennai Metro recorded the highest flip rate at 94%
- Delta was the most stable region at 45% flip rate
### Research Questions Answered
1. Constituency-level party flips between 2021 and 2026
2. Regional patterns of electoral change
3. Party realignment — who gained from the shift
